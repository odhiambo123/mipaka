Western Bahr El Ghazal Community –USA Humanitarian Program

In the war-torn country of South Sudan, the largest refugee crisis in Africa and the third-largest in the world looms. More than 1.87 million South Sudanese remain internally displaced in their country, while more than 2.3 million have been forced to flee to neighboring nations.

To help solve the humanitarian crisis, South Sudanese American citizens through their Community base Organization that have established as a non-profit organization registered in Phoenix Arizona, Western Bahr El Ghazal Community. The organization has actively been involved in providing humanitarian aid to the internally displaced persons and the refugees in the neighboring country of Sudan. Amongst the most vulnerable and affected persons are children, women, and the elderly. Western Bahr El Ghazal Community is assisting these people by providing food, drinking water, shelter, medications, and clothing. Specifically, the organization is supporting the school going children by paying their tuition as well as providing them with money to cater for their transport to school and purchasing books. In efforts to raise awareness and funds to help the needy South Sudanese citizens, Western Bahr El Ghazal Community ran the biggest ever campaign in the United States known as CARE PACKAGES in Lincoln Nebraska. The campaign was able to collect two giant containers full of medical equipment, clothing, snacks, and toys for refugee children in South Sudan and Sudan. However, the organization has been unable to deliver the aid due to inadequate funds to cater for the transport and distribution.

It is due to this that the Western Bahr Ghazal Community is appealing to well-wishers to support this noble mission and show love to humanity by supporting the people of South Sudan. We at Western Bahr Ghazal Community believe that your help will help make a difference in improving the lives of the people of South Sudan.
